1. How many planets are there in the solar system?
   A. 7
   B. 8
   C. 9
   D. 10
   Correct answer: B. 8

2. Which planet is known for its rings?
   A. Jupiter
   B. Saturn
   C. Mars
   D. Venus
   Correct answer: B. Saturn

3. Which planet is closest to the Sun?
   A. Earth
   B. Venus
   C. Mercury
   D. Mars
   Correct answer: C. Mercury

4. Which planet is known as the "Red Planet"?
   A. Jupiter
   B. Saturn
   C. Mars
   D. Uranus
   Correct answer: C. Mars

5. Which planet is the largest in the solar system?
   A. Earth
   B. Jupiter
   C. Saturn
   D. Neptune
   Correct answer: B. Jupiter
