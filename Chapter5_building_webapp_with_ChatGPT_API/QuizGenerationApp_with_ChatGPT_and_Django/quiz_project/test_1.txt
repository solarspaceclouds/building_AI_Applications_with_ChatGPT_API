Text: 

"The Great Gatsby" is a novel written by F. Scott Fitzgerald and published in 1925. The story is set in the fictional town of West Egg on Long Island and follows the life of Jay Gatsby, a wealthy and mysterious man who throws lavish parties in the hopes of winning back his lost love, Daisy Buchanan.

Questions:

1. What is the title of the novel written by F. Scott Fitzgerald in 1925?
   A. The Catcher in the Rye
   B. The Great Gatsby
   C. To Kill a Mockingbird
   D. Pride and Prejudice
   Correct Answer: B. The Great Gatsby

2. Where is the story of "The Great Gatsby" set?
   A. New York City
   B. Los Angeles
   C. West Egg on Long Island
   D. Chicago
   Correct Answer: C. West Egg on Long Island

3. Who is the main character in "The Great Gatsby"?
   A. Nick Carraway
   B. Daisy Buchanan
   C. Jay Gatsby
   D. Tom Buchanan
   Correct Answer: C. Jay Gatsby

4. What does Jay Gatsby hope to achieve by throwing lavish parties?
   A. To become famous
   B. To win a bet
   C. To impress his friends
   D. To win back Daisy Buchanan
   Correct Answer: D. To win back Daisy Buchanan

5. What is the relationship between Jay Gatsby and Daisy Buchanan?
   A. Siblings
   B. Cousins
   C. Former lovers
   D. Friends
   Correct Answer: C. Former lovers
