Text:

"The Great Gatsby" is a novel written by F. Scott Fitzgerald and published in 1925. The story follows the lives of Jay Gatsby, a mysterious millionaire, and his love interest, Daisy Buchanan, in the fictional town of West Egg, Long Island.

Questions:

1. What is the title of the novel written by F. Scott Fitzgerald in 1925?
   A. The Catcher in the Rye
   B. The Great Gatsby
   C. To Kill a Mockingbird
   D. Pride and Prejudice
   
   Correct Answer: B. The Great Gatsby
   
2. Who is the mysterious millionaire in the novel?
   A. Tom Buchanan
   B. Nick Carraway
   C. Jay Gatsby
   D. George Wilson
   
   Correct Answer: C. Jay Gatsby
   
3. Where is the fictional town of West Egg located?
   A. California
   B. New York
   C. Florida
   D. Texas
   
   Correct Answer: B. New York
   
4. Who is Jay Gatsby's love interest in the novel?
   A. Myrtle Wilson
   B. Daisy Buchanan
   C. Jordan Baker
   D. Catherine McKee
   
   Correct Answer: B. Daisy Buchanan
